This is my website. I am having difficulties putting it together, but what else is new?

Several years ago, I read an article posted by Lawrence Evalyn on The Toast, which highlighted some of the recurring motifs appearing in early Gothic novels. The source of this article was Ann B. Tracy's "The Gothic Novel 1790-1830: Plot Summaries and Index to Motifs." Happily, I was able to find a copy of this book at the UCLA Library, and instantly admired the level of dedication it took to summarize and categorize over 200 separate works. I was interested in translating the motif index into a digital form which could then be used to visualize some of these trends.

The author gave me permission to upload my digitized index to Harvard Dataverse, so that interested users might be able to make their own visualizations with this incredible resource.
